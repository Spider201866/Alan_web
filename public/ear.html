All around ear

Check: pinna, tragus, mastoid for lumps, tenderness or discharge Gently move pinna, note any pain

Ear canal

Tilt head, hold Arclight like a pen Pull pinna up/back (adults) or down/back (children) Insert speculum (4.5mm adult, 2.5mm infant), push past hairs, rotate if needed Look for: wax, debris, infection

Tympanic membrane

Identify handle of malleus, light reflex, attic Note: color, position, translucency Look for perforation, fluid or scarring
Know your TM's: normal, red, bulging, retracted, perforated Practice often
