Front of Eye

Observe and compare eyes: straight, right, left, up, down Hold & get close. Examine: lids, conjunctiva, cornea, pupil Use with fluro for corneal ulcers or scratches

Back of Eye

Use right eye to view patient's right eye; left for left Patient must look straight NOT at light; get close and find optic disc (Dilate = best view) Study disc: Margin, Colour, Cup. Track big vessels, then ask patient to look directly into the light to see macula
Know your discs: normal, swollen, new vessels, cupped, pale >Practice often<
